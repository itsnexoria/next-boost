Why bought Instagram followers hurt more than they help

It's tempting to pay for a quick follower boost, but Instagram's algorithm weighs engagement rate heavily when deciding whether to show your posts to more people. A follower count full of inactive or fake accounts drags that rate down, which means even your real followers see less of your content. Worse, mass-bought followers can get your account flagged for suspicious activity.
